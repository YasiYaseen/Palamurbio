Palamur Biosciences (p) Ltd ( PBS ) established in the year 2008 is a full service Preclinical Contract Research Organization (CRO) based out sub-urban of HYDERABAD, INDIA. PBS has as a centre of excellence for undertaking GLP toxicology / safety assessments, exploratory and basic research studies.


We have an integrated facility with various Research Models, Systems, Infrastructures and the Scientists as a one stop preclinical testing facility. Our extensive range of Research Models including the large animals like Beagle dogs, Mini Pigs (also conventional pigs) and sheep etc., and also its state-of-the-art infrastructure and supports, Pharma, Medical Devices, Biotech, Pesticide industries.
